§ 03.5 FOUNDATION / RODEO · DEEP DIVE

A closer look at two flagship workstreams from my time at Foundation — one consumer activation that pushed the boundaries of onchain minting UX, and the platform product work that defined Rodeo.club's social layer.

COINBASE ONCHAIN SUMMER 2024

Babes by Petra Voice

I led the Babes project — one of the first high-profile campaigns to support the Coinbase Smart Wallet during Onchain Summer 2024.

29.3K
NFTs minted
7.1K
Unique holders
51%
Conversion rate

WHAT MADE IT DIFFERENT

  • ERC-1155 PFPs — multi-edition collectibles, not the typical 1-of-1 PFP drop. Each Babe was a distinct AI-generated character with unique traits.
  • AI-generated PFPs — the art itself was produced through an AI pipeline, making each piece feel personal while maintaining the collection's visual coherence.
  • Multi-mint packs — users could mint in bundles instead of one-at-a-time, dramatically improving conversion.
  • "Cart" experience — a first for onchain minting: users could browse, favorite, and add Babes to a cart before minting — collapsing the typical mint flow into something that felt like shopping.

FOUNDATION · RODEO.CLUB

Rodeo

I helped lead the product that turned Foundation's social sketchbook into a Farcaster-native creative platform on Base.

FEATURES & INTEGRATIONS I LED

  • Bypassed gate period — connected Rodeo's onboarding directly to Farcaster, letting FC users skip the waitlist entirely.
  • Social graph imports — connected users to people they already knew by importing their Farcaster social graph into Rodeo on sign-up.
  • Farcaster Frames for minting — built mint-ready Frames so any Rodeo post could be collected directly from the Farcaster feed, no app switch needed.
  • Top 9 — a shareable OpenGraph embed of your 9 most recent posts, auto-tagging the creators and spreading discovery across Farcaster.
  • Mentions, comments & threads — built the social layer: @mentions that notify and link profiles, threaded replies, and a comment system that made every post a conversation.
  • Tastemaker rewards — a reward system where your own mint activity influenced what others collected. Early supporters of breakout pieces earned for having good taste.
  • Trust & Safety — moderation tooling, content policies, and safety infrastructure for a social platform where every post is an NFT.
